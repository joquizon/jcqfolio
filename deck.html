CLUB DIAMOND PLAYING CARDS
context:
Graphic Design Class proj. 2013


The Game assignment required the designer to produce a package for a game. The designer chose the traditional deck of cards for redesign. The concept cards produced were meant to exude a contemporary look that did not stray too far from standard playing card requirements. The packaging's minimal look was meant to refer back to the bars used in the card designs, while its opening was inspired by the classic zippo lighter design.
